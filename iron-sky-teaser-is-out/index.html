Iron Sky teaser is out!

A teaser trailer for Iron Sky, the next film from the makers of Star Wreck, is out at ironsky.net . I loved it! The best part of it all is the community behind the project. Anyone can join the project in form of ideas, concept art or whatever for the movie. Join the cause at Wreck a Movie ! Another way to help the process is buying War Bonds at the Iron Sky website. For 50 euros you get a bunch of promotional stuff and help the movie.

The numbers added up it seems they are getting one 25th of the budget by selling 2000 sets of war bonds. Seems like a good way to collect funds. Let's see what the business model is going to be like when the first community-driven movie is coming out.
